provide a vent area of about 40 percent but not less than 30 percent of the unobstructed area of the stream 									
measured between the proposed road top level and the stream bed.However, the available area of flow									
under design HFL condition should always be at least 70 percent of the unobstructed area of flow between 									
the design HFL and the stream bed i.e. the obstruction under design HFL condition should not be more 									
than 30per cent.
